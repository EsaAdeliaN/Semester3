Food Recipes for CKD Sufferers


Grouper

Ingredients

  • 3 sliced garlic cloves
  • 2 sliced ginger segments
  • 2 tbs soy sauce
  • 1 Grouper
  • 2 pcs lime juice
  • 1 tbs salt

How to make

  • Soak the fish with lime juice, sprinkle with sliced garlic, sprinkle with sliced ginger
  • Steam fish until cooked 25-30 minutes
  • Pour water into a new pan, add soy sauce and salt
  • Raise fish
  • Water the fish with cooked soy sauce
